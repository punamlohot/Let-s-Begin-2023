Headphones are a pair of small loudspeaker drivers worn on or around the head over a user's ears. They are electroacoustic transducers,
which convert an electrical signal to a corresponding sound. Headphones let a single user listen to an audio source privately, in contrast
to a loudspeaker, which emits sound into the open air for anyone nearby to hear.
